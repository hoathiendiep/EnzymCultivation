PhD. Diep Quynh Nhu

I am a researcher in the field of bioethanol production, enzyme activities and applying bio-products (enzyme, microorganism) in environmental treatment and food processing using enzyme with 20 years of experience. My major is Biochemistry, I was granted a Doctor degree by Hiroshima University, Japan in 2014. I used to work at Institute of Tropical Biology, VAST (2000-2007), Biomass Technology Research Center-AIST, Hiroshima, Japan (2008-2012),株式会社建設環境研究所 , Tokyo (2012-2016). Recently I have successfully developed an enzyme cocktail for pretreatment of sawdust. I have a keen interest in promoting the use of this product in mushroom cultivation, boosting the use of other enzymes, probiotics products in environmental treatment, Agriculture, Aquaculture, Food processing, Husbandairy sectors and be an active technical consultant based on my expertise.
