    PhD. Diep Quynh Nhu

    I am a researcher in the field of bioethanol production, enzyme solutions in woody biomass processing with over 20 years of experience. My major is Biochemistry, I was granted a Doctor degree by Hiroshima University, Japan in 2014. I used to work at Institute of Tropical Biology, VAST (2000-2007), Biomass Technology Research Center-AIST, Hiroshima, Japan (2008-2012), 株式会社建設環境研究所, Tokyo (2012-2016). Recently I have successfully created an enzyme formula for pre-treatment of cellulosic biomass (sawdust, cotton,…). Promoting the use of this enzyme formula in mushroom cultivation is my top concern for a remarkable technical progress in Mushroom industry in Vietnam. Besides, I have been a pioneer in enzyme solutions in a wide range of sectors, especially in Environmental treatment, Agriculture, Aquaculture, Food processing, Husbandry sectors and be an active technical consultant based on my expertise.

    Msc. Diep Le Chi

    I am a lecturer at the Quang Binh University with over 14 years of experience in teaching and doing research in husbandry sector. In 2012, I was granted Master of Science degree by Hue University, since then I have done several studies related to applying enzyme, probiotics in animal feeds, livestock-environmental treatment, especially converting animal waste manure to value-added products such as compost fertilizer. Using enzyme, probiotics products as an additive in livestock feed and environmental treatment is an advanced technique, with many benefits. In a short time of digestion in intestine, additional enzymes from feed break down (hydrolyze) the food and help livestock to absorb maximum nutrients, save the amount of feedings, reduce bad odors, and alleviate the environmental pollution. Undoubtedly, applying enzymes, probiotics products in husbandry sector is inevitable for sustainable development of Agriculture in the future.
